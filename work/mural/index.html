Why

For the last year I’ve worked at MURAL, where our mission is to enable creative people around the world to be more productive and collaborative. We believe that diverse teams lead to better outcomes, and the widest diversity is usually beyond one physical location. We also believe that a creative process that is inclusive and visual makes teams more successful.

Those values make up the core philosophy behind our product: online, collaborative whiteboards called murals that people use to brainstorm with distributed teams, facilitate workshops with international participants, build diagrams collaboratively and much more. In 2017, our users created ~300K murals and each week more than 20K people from across the globe used our product.

Role

As a product designer, I worked with PMs, engineering, customer success and sales to identify problems and opportunities in people’s experience using MURAL and design solutions for them.
