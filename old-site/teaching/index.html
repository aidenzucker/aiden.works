Skillshare Collective
2014-2017
    Skillshare Collective is an organization that I cofounded with the mission to connect typically separate communities through teaching. We’ve organized over 100 workshops from sushi-rolling to salsa dancing to bookbinding to geopolitics.
Philosophy

    I believe that anybody can and should teach things that they love, and that you don’t truly understand something unless you can teach it.

    Reach out if you’re interested in partnering on anything educational related to design thinking, product design, rapid prototyping, creative confidence or language learning.
