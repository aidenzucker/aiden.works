Work Info

Instead of saying that I’m a "fun sock enthusiast" and "cheese lover," I’m just going to tell you my story.

I’m Aiden. I grew up in the Bay Area. When I was 16 I participated in a homestay and youth leadership program in Nicaragua. Two years later, I became a project supervisor for the same program, where I realized my obsession for working on tough problems with a collaborative, diverse and transparent team.

In college I worked summers at IBM Design in Austin and Start-Up Chile in Santiago. I graduated with a BFA in Communication Design from Washington University in St. Louis.

After college I moved back to Chile and designed independently for a few startups. At the beginning of 2017 I moved to Buenos Aires, Argentina to work at a software startup called MURAL, where I spent a year learning from some of the smartest people I’ve worked with.

My biggest motivator is learning and my biggest fear is complacency. Currently I am traveling between Latin America and California and looking for my next big project. If you’ve read this far and have something interesting you’re working on, I’d love to hear from you.
