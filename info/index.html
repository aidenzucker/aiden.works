About

I grew up in the Bay Area. As a teenager I spent two summers in Nicaragua and Peru working for a homestay and youth leadership organization, where I learned how fulfilling it can be to work in a collaborative, diverse and transparent team.

In college I worked at IBM Design in Austin and Start-Up Chile in Santiago. I graduated with a BFA in Communication Design from Washington University in St. Louis.

After college I moved back to Chile and designed independently for several startups. In 2017 I moved to Buenos Aires, Argentina to work for a software startup called MURAL. I’m fascinated with the problems around remote collaboration and it remains the most meaningful and challenging work I’ve done.
